Whispering Spirit

Whispering Spirit is a new company that sells dream catcher horseshoes. The company was started by Laura Gordon because she want to give a friend something to remember her horse by so she took one of the shoes and decided to make a dreamcetcher out of it. Because she wanted the horse to still protect her and this way it keeps all of the bad dreams out and the good dream in. and after that a lot of people started to complementing her on the shoe so she decided to start making them and selling the and small art shows and from there it just took of.
